Solar World

Solar World is a mmo rpg game, where you can fight in dungeons! You can hire dungeons to fight with 4 friends or more. In dungeons you can fight enemies, but not only that, you can mine crystals which are worth a ton of in game money. You can gain stats randomly and a set rank according to your stats. This game has perma death, so if you die you lose all your stuff, but of course not stats. (because if you were to lose your stats the algorithm would give you new stats which would be unfair). Classes are gained by how well you do in a test, you get this test in the tutorial, but how good you do in this determine what class and what rank of that class you will get. There's only one way to get a class change and that's if you find the rare spawned dungeon called the dual dungeon, which on rare occassions can spawn in another dungeon, when this dungeon is completed, the people who've completed will gain the "Player" role, this means they can level up their stats, hold items in inventory and do daily quest and stuff like that. Becoming a player allows you to spawn dungeon portal and open up new dungeons. *Note* These dungeons are solo dungeons, which means you can't do them with friends. Make sure not to die! The strength of an enemies is found with the colour of the enemies name, if it's red that means he's really strong, white means he's not, orange is in between etc. Anyways click on the image below to direct you to the game's page. Have fun! (P.S the game is currently still in work in progress)
